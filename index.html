All heroes and their abilities:


Magmax:

Flow: Boosts speed. Costs 7 energy/second. (+1.5/3/4.5/6/7.5 speed)

Harden: Gives invulnerability, but also stops movement. Costs 15 energy/second.(2.5/2/1.5/1/0.5 cooldown)


Rime

Warp: Teleports player in target direction. Costs 10 energy/second.(80/95/110/125/140 range) 0.3s cooldown.

Paralysis: Freeze enemies for 2 seconds. Costs 15 energy.(130/145/160/175/190 range)


Morfe

Reverse: Shoots projectiles that repels enemies away. Costs 15 energy. 2 second cooldown. (2/3/4/5/6 projectiles)

Minimize: Reduces enemies' speed and radius by 50%. Costs 15 energy. 2 second cooldown. (2/3/4/5/6 projectiles)


Aurora

Distort: Slows enemies within aura by 30/35/40/45/50%. Costs 11 energy/second.(130/150/170/190/210 range)

Energize: Gives a burst of unlimited energy and cooldown reduction to allies. Costs 30 energy. 10 second cooldown. (2/3/4/5/6 seconds)


Necro

Resurrection: Revives self. 1 use per 100 pellets.

Reanimate: Shoots a projectile that revives teammates on hit. Costs 30 energy. (1/2/3/4/5 projectiles)(14/12/10/8/6 cooldown)


Brute

Stomp: Sends enemies flying back and stuns for 4s. Costs 20 energy. 1 second cooldown. (100/125/130/145/160 range)

Vigor: Reduces enemy effects by 15/30/45/60/75% but increases radius by 3/3/6/6/9%


Nexus

Barrier: Places a dome that grants invulnerability. Costs 30 energy. 6 second cooldown. Lasts 2.5/2.7/2.9/3.1/3.3s

Stream: Places a path that provides a speed boost. Costs 20 energy. 3s cooldown. 600/800/1000/1200/1400 length. +3/3.5/4/4.5/5 speed.
